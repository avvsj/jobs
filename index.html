Freelance Site Engineer / Setting Out Engineer

We are currently supplying Site Engineers to active UK projects and are seeking experienced freelance engineers for immediate and upcoming assignments.

Location UK (Birmingham, London & Nationwide)
Job Type Freelance / Contract / Self-Employed
Rate Starting from GBP 200 per day

Key Responsibilities

  • Setting out using Total Station, Dumpy Level and Laser.
  • Carry out as-built surveys and QA checks.
  • Review technical drawings and site information.
  • Maintain QA records and site documentation.
  • Support site health & safety compliance.

Requirements

  • Valid CSCS Card.
  • SMSTS / SSSTS preferred.
  • First Aid preferred.
  • UK experience as Site Engineer / Setting Out Engineer.
  • Right to work in the UK.
  • Available on freelance / self-employed basis.
